Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

What data we collect

ToSDigest does not collect any of your personal data. All processing happens in your browser or on our hosted summarizer API. We only send the text of the Terms of Service you’re viewing, and no identifying information (no cookies, no IP logs).

How we use it

We forward the ToS text to OpenAI’s API for summarization, and then display the result. We do not store your ToS or any other data long-term.

Third-party services

We use the OpenAI API under our server to perform summarization. Please review OpenAI’s own privacy terms for how they handle text you send them.
